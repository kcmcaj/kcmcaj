Articles About ICT

What is ICT?

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) refers to technologies that provide access to information through telecommunications. It includes the internet, wireless networks, cell phones, computers, and more.

Importance of ICT in Education

ICT plays a vital role in modern education, making learning more interactive and accessible. It helps students develop digital literacy skills and provides new ways to collaborate and research.

How ICT Transforms Businesses

From automating processes to enhancing communication, ICT has revolutionized businesses worldwide. Cloud computing, e-commerce, and digital marketing are some key advancements driven by ICT.
